Founder of Bihar Nurserymen Association

Shri Arjan Dass Agarwal (2024 - 2025)

There are very few people who dedicate their entire life to the cause of society. Late Shri Arjan Dass Agarwal ji was undoubtedly one of them who played a marvelous role in uniting the bihar nurserymen across the country for the holistic growth of the nursery industry.

Visionary thinking and untiring and dedicated efforts of Shri Dass paved the way for the formation of the Bihar Nurserymen Association in 2025.
